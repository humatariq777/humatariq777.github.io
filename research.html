How Perceptions of Organizational Politics Influence Employee Outcomes

MPhil Thesis · 2022 · Huma Tariq

This thesis investigates how employees’ perceptions of organizational politics influence turnover intention and counterproductive work behavior. The study proposes workplace ostracism as a mediating mechanism and hostile attribution bias as a moderator, drawing on Social Exchange Theory.

Data were collected from private-sector employees, and the model was tested using moderated mediation analysis.
